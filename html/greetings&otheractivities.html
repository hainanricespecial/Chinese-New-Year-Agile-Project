Dumplings Dumplings are extremely famous in Northern China. It is one of the fundamental dishes for New Year's Eve supper. Alternately, not very many individuals in Southern China serve dumplings in New Year's Eve supper.



New Year Cake It is strong cake made with glutinous rice flour along with some sugar. New Year Cake is famous in Eastern China.



Tang Yuan Tang Yuan is little ball produced using glutinous rice flour. Glutinous rice flour is blended in with a modest quantity of water to frame balls and is then cooked and served in bubbling water. Tang Yuan can be either filled or unfilled. It is generally eaten during Yuan Xiao, or the Lantern Festival (the fifteenth of the primary month of the conventional Chinese schedule).



LaBa Congee This is a combination of rice, nuts, and beans cooked together. LaBa Congee is typically served at the LaBa celebration, which is the eighth day of the last month of the year.
